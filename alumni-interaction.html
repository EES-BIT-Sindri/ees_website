Alumni Interaction Team

The Alumni Interaction team of the Electrical Engineering Society at BIT Sindri is a dedicated group of individuals responsible for fostering engagement and collaboration between the society's current students and the alumni who have graduated from the Electrical Engineering program at BIT Sindri.

  1. Home
  2. Alumni Interaction Team

The Alumni Interaction team at BIT Sindri's Electrical Engineering Society facilitates student-alumni connections through database management, networking events, mentorship, speaker series, industry visits, fundraising, and communication channels. This strengthens a supportive community for mutual personal and professional growth.


The team's primary objective is to create a strong network that benefits both the students and the alumni. Here is a comprehensive guide on the responsibilities and key activities of the Alumni Interaction team:

  • Alumni Database Management: The Alumni Interaction team manages an alumni database containing contact info, professional details, and achievements of Electrical Engineering graduates from BIT Sindri, fostering future alumni-student interactions and collaborations.
  • Alumni Networking Events: The team arranges networking events like alumni reunions, industry meetups, guest lectures, and panel discussions, connecting students with alumni for mentorship and career prospects.
  • Alumni Mentoring Program: The team aims to create a mentoring program connecting alumni with students who share common interests, career goals, or academic aspirations. They will oversee recruitment, matching, and mentorship meetings.
  • Alumni Speaker Series: The team organizes talks and presentations by accomplished electrical engineering alumni, offering valuable insights, industry trends, and inspiration to students. They handle speaker invitations, event logistics, and promotion.
  • Alumni Fundraising and Sponsorship: The team plans to involve alumni in fundraising efforts to support the society's activities and projects. They will also explore alumni sponsorship for specific events and competitions, taking charge of fundraising opportunities and campaigns.
  • Alumni Communication and Newsletter:The team stays connected with alumni through newsletters, emails, social media, and online platforms, sharing society updates, events, and student engagement opportunities while also managing these communication channels.
