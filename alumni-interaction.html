Alumni Interaction Team

The Alumni Interaction team of the Electrical Engineering Society at BIT Sindri is a dedicated group of individuals responsible for fostering engagement and collaboration between the society's current students and the alumni who have graduated from the Electrical Engineering program at BIT Sindri.

  2. Alumni Interaction Team

The Alumni Interaction team of the Electrical Engineering Society at BIT Sindri plays a crucial role in fostering engagement and collaboration between the society's current students and alumni. By maintaining an alumni database, organizing networking events, establishing a mentoring program, arranging alumni speaker series, organizing industry visits, engaging alumni in fundraising and sponsorship, and maintaining regular communication channels with alumni through newsletters, emails, social media groups, and dedicated online platforms, the team helps to create a vibrant and supportive community that benefits both the students and the alumni in their personal and professionalÂ growth.


The team's primary objective is to create a strong network that benefits both the students and the alumni. Here is a comprehensive guide on the responsibilities and key activities of the Alumni Interaction team:

  • Alumni Database Management: The Alumni Interaction team is responsible for maintaining an alumni database that includes contact information, professional details, and achievements of all alumni who have graduated from the Electrical Engineering program at BIT Sindri. This database serves as a valuable resource for future interactions and collaborations between the alumni and current students.
  • Alumni Networking Events: The team organizes various networking events such as alumni reunions, industry-specific meetups, guest lectures, and panel discussions. These events provide opportunities for students to connect with alumni, learn from their experiences, and explore potential mentorship or career opportunities. The team is responsible for planning, promoting, and executing these events.
  • Alumni Mentoring Program: The team may establish a mentoring program where alumni are paired with interested students based on their areas of interest, career aspirations, or academic goals. This program allows students to receive guidance, advice, and support from experienced professionals in the field. The team is responsible for recruiting alumni mentors, matching them with interested students, and facilitating regular meetings between mentors and mentees.
  • Alumni Speaker Series: The team arranges talks and presentations by accomplished alumni who have made significant contributions to the field of electrical engineering. These sessions provide valuable insights, industry trends, and real-world examples to inspire and educate students. The team is responsible for identifying and inviting alumni speakers, coordinating logistics for the events, and promoting the sessions to the student community.
  • Alumni Fundraising and Sponsorship: The team may engage alumni in fundraising initiatives to support the society's activities, projects, or events. Additionally, they may seek sponsorship from alumni for specific initiatives or competitions organized by the society. The team is responsible for identifying fundraising opportunities, creating fundraising campaigns, and engaging alumni in these initiatives.
  • Alumni Communication and Newsletter: The team maintains regular communication channels with alumni through newsletters, emails, social media groups, and dedicated online platforms. These platforms keep alumni informed about society updates, events, and opportunities to engage with the current student community. The team is responsible for creating and distributing these communications, as well as managing the online platforms used for communication.
